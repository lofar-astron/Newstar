Program DWARF: private keyword USERLEVEL

Prompt: BEGINNER, AVERAGE, EXPERT How do you rate yourself as a DWARF user
Default: BEGINNER.
Expected input: Character(8).

Defines the amount of information to be given with parameter prompts:

EXPERT: Keyword name and default only AVERAGE: Same plus available options where applicable BEGINNER: Some descriptive information in addition While being prompted, you may temporarily descend to a "lower" level by typing a question mark; by adding /KEEP to the "?" you may retain this lower level until program exit.

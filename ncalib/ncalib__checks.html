Program NCALIB: private keyword CHECKS

Prompt: Iterations, gain deviation, relative mean error: 3 values|
Default: 20, 1E-3, 3.
Expected input: Real number, 3 values; min.value: 5.000000; max.value: 50.000000.

Specify:

- the maximum number of iterations in the complex solution;

- the relative allowable gain deviation for successive complex solutions;

- the mean error allowed per scan, relative to the average mean error for all scans already solved.

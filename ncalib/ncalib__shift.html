Program NCALIB: private keyword SHIFT

Prompt: Shift to de-apply to data (arcsec)
Expected input: Real number, 2 values.

Specify the l, m shift in arcsec to be applied to data whenever the DE_APPLY SHIFT correction is requested. (Ignore the 'de-apply) in the prompt.)

In the case where the shift is time-dependent (e.g. for a planet) it is approximated by a linear function

Total shift = SHIFT + DSHIFT * (HA-HAB)

In this case the SHIFT you specify here is the value at the meridian, DSHIFT is a second parameter for which you will be prompted. ! \whichref{}{} !! HA-HAB or just HA?

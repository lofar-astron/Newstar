Program NCLEAN: private keyword PRUSSIAN_HAT

Prompt: Prussian hat value
Expected input: Real number; min.value: 0.000000; max.value: 0.500000.

For extended sources a prussian hat (i.e. a nominal additional peak value on the central value of the antenna pattern) may give better clean results. Values of .1 to .4 could be tried.

Program NGCALC: private keyword POLY_COEF

Prompt: polynomial coefficients
Expected input: Real number, 11 values.

Specify the coefficients of the polynomial to be subtracted:

P= c0 +c1*HA +c2*(HA**2) +... +cN*(HA**N)

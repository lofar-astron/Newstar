Program NGCALC: private keyword POLY_USE

Prompt: orders of coefficients to fit
Expected input: Integer number, 11 values.

Specify the orders of the polynomial coefficients to fit to. Default is 0, ..., <N>, where <N> is the number you gave for POLY_N.

Example: To fit a 6th-degree even polynomial, specify POLY_N=3 and POLY_USE=0, 2, 4, 6.

Program NGCALC: private keyword USE_NGF_SET

Prompt: Cut to use
Expected input: Character(32), 1 values.

Define the cut represented by each of the variables of the form #<n> and ##<n> in your expression. You are being prompted for each such variable that is present in the expression. Your reply must designate ONE cut.

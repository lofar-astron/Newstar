Description of general keywords (NGFSETS)

  • NGF_SETS Sectors to do: grp.fld.chn.pol.iort.seq
  • NGF_LOOPS Loop specifications: nr of cycles, index increment per cycle
  • NGF_GROUPS 1st index: grp = groups
  • NGF_FIELDS 2nd index: field(s)
  • NGF_CHANNELS 3rd index: channel(s)
  • NGF_POLARS 4th index: polarisation(s)
  • NGF_IFRS 5th index: iort = interferometers or telescopes
  • NGF_CUTS 6th index: cut(s)

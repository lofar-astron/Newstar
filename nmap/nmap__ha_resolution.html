Program NMAP: private keyword HA_RESOLUTION

Prompt: Hour-angle averaging interval (UT seconds)
Default: .50137.
Expected input: Real number; min.value: 0.040000; max.value: 15.000000.

Specify the width in UT degrees of hour angle over which visibilities will be averaged (to reduce the noise per plotted point).

Note: Observations are taken at multiples of 10 UT seconds and it is therefore convenient to specify this parameter in UT seconds as well. The number you specify will be converted to a sidereal hour-angle interval.

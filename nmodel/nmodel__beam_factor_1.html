Description of general keyword BEAM_FACTOR_1

Prompt: Beam feed factors
Default: 8.99E-4, 2.15E-6, -2.23E-09, 1.56E-12, 1.02E-3, 9.48E-7, -3.68E-10, 4.88E-13, - 1.08E-3, 1.31E-6, -1.17E-09, 1.07E-12, - 1.04E-3, 8.36E-7, -4.68E-10, 5.50E-13 /NOASK.
Expected input: Real number, 64 values.

Specify per frequency band specified in BEAM_FREQ_1 the factors to be used in the primary beam calculation formula (see BEAM_DESCR). This is for ATCA 1/(1+ai.x**2i)

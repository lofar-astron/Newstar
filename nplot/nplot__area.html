Program NPLOT: private keyword AREA

Prompt: Area centre (l, m) and width (dl, dm) in grid units
Expected input: Integer number, 4 values.

Specify an area of a map:

l, m grid coordinates of area centre: 0, 0 is the map centre, increasing to the upper right (i.e. with DEcreasing right ascension and INcreasing declination)

dl, dm area width and height

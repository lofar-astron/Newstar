Description of general keywords (SCNSETS)

  • SCN_SETS Sectors to process: grp.obs.fld.chn.seq
  • m of all Loop specifications: |- nr of cycles, index increment (grp.obs.fld.chn.seq) per cycle |
  • OVERVIEW OBS, ALTOBS, FLD, CHN, SCT Detail level for overview
  • SCN_GROUPS 1st index: grp = groups
  • SCN_OBSS 2nd index: observations
  • SCN_FIELDS 3rd index: fields
  • SCN_CHANNELS 4th index: channels
  • SCN_SECTORS 5th index: sectors

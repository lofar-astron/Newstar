The Netherlands East West Synthesis Telescope Array Reduction

Newstar is the software package to reduce data from the WSRT, the Westerbork Synthese Radio Telescope.

The WSRT is operated by the Netherlands Foundation for Research in Astronomy (the NFRA).

If you have questions, bugs or requests, please contact us through our User Feedback System, by eMail to newstar@astron.nl or devoscm@nfra, or by calling Marco de Vos (NFRA) at 0521 595247.

Program IONOST: private keyword DAY_NUMBER

Prompt:
Default: 0 /ASK.
Expected input: Integer number.

day number for which the calculations have to be done. The format is as YYMMDD, where YY is the year - 1900, MM the month number in the year and DD the daynumber in the month. Several dates may be specified: Specify after the last day wanted a "0" (= zero) - then the program will exit after the last calculations needed.

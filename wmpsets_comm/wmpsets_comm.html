Description of general keywords (WMPSETS)

  • WMP_SETS Images to process: grp.fld.chn.pol.typ.seq |
  • can be used as an alternative. Loop specifications: |- nr of cycles, index increment (grp.fld.chn.pol.typ.seq) per cycle |
  • WMP_GROUPS 1st index: grp = groups
  • WMP_FIELDS 2nd index: field(s)
  • WMP_CHANNELS 3rd index: channel(s)
  • WMP_POLARS 4th index: polarisation(s)
  • WMP_TYPES 5th index: typ = map (0) or antenna (1)
  • WMP_MAPS 6th index: image sequence number(s)
